## Purpose

A calm, player-first sleep and wind-down companion: natural soundscapes, bedtime stories / narrator voices, breathing, streaks, sleep timer, shareable mixes, and offline-friendly PWA behavior. Preferences are local-first today (no accounts).

Live demo for learning — free educational showcase under MIT.

## Strategic role

Move the public story from “AI helped build an app” to “AI helped transform a vague product idea into a deployed, maintainable product.” Emphasize product thinking, UX, architecture, engineering process, and shared-pattern potential under the **AI in Action** parent brand shared with RetireCheck.
