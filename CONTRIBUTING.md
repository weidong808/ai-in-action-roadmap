# Contributing

Thank you for interest in the **AI in Action** roadmap. This repository holds public planning and documentation only — not application source code.

## What belongs here

- Roadmap and vision updates
- Architecture and strategy docs for the **educational / showcase** portfolio
- Mermaid diagrams
- Milestone plans and decision log entries
- Issue templates for product/architecture discussion

## What does not belong here

- Application source code (use [Retirement-Calculator](https://github.com/weidong808/Retirement-Calculator) or [SleepCheck](https://github.com/weidong808/SleepCheck))
- Secrets or credentials
- Private commercial strategy, pricing, payment providers, or business plans
- Marketing hype or unsupported medical claims

See [docs/scope.md](./docs/scope.md).

## How to propose a change

1. Open an issue using a template when possible:
   - [Feature request](./.github/ISSUE_TEMPLATE/feature-request.md)
   - [Architecture decision](./.github/ISSUE_TEMPLATE/architecture-decision.md)
2. For docs PRs, keep changes incremental and distinguish **current state** from **future vision**.
3. Update [docs/decision-log.md](./docs/decision-log.md) when a meaningful decision is made.
4. Prefer linking to live apps and app repos rather than duplicating implementation detail.

## Tone and quality bar

- Practical and phased — no vaporware
- Explicit assumptions
- Preserve licensing, privacy, and wellness positioning
- Align with [VISION.md](./VISION.md) and [AGENTS.md](./AGENTS.md)
- Frame optional future work as **engineering learning**, not commercial packaging

## AI-assisted contributions

AI tools may help draft docs. Humans remain accountable for accuracy, licensing notes, and privacy claims. Do not invent live URLs, compliance status, or “production readiness” without evidence.

**Agents:** Do not add monetization, pricing, payment providers, or private business strategy to this public repository.
